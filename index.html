About Us

Afghan Cuisine

Afghan cuisine is a rich tapestry of flavors, deeply rooted in tradition and influenced by its neighbors. Dishes often feature rice, meat (especially lamb), vegetables, and a variety of aromatic spices. Some popular examples include Kabuli Pulao, a fragrant rice dish with lamb and raisins, and Bolani, a stuffed flatbread. Other notable dishes include Mantu (dumplings), Ashak (leek dumplings), and various kebab varieties.
